## Format Check Categories
1. Page size (A4)
2. Body text font/size (SimSun 12pt)
3. Chapter heading font/size (SimHei 22pt)
4. Section heading font/size (SimHei 16pt/14pt)
5. Figure/table/equation numbering continuity
6. Reference numbering continuity
7. Page headers
8. Page numbers
